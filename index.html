CIE Raw Mark Convertor

The CIE Raw Mark Convertor is a tool that takes your CIE mark, along with grade thresholds to return the actual number of marks an indivudal has received.
I decided to make this after realising that learning my raw marks could be benificial for my own knowledge, however the formula needed to do so (alhtough not difficult) requires many steps. Through the site, I am able to calculate the marks faster for myself and others within an accuracy of 1 mark.

ASTROBLASTER


ASTROBLASTER is a 2d top down space shooter game made in Unity C#. The game uses a joystick for movement and another button to shoot as its main form of control. The objective is to shoot as many asteroids down before one of them hits you and causes you to lose. Asteroids are generated from a locus of points equidistant from the centre of the camera, and their direction is choosen at random from a point on the visible screen.
